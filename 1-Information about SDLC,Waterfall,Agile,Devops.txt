--------------what is waterfall model------------------------------------
waterfall model is a linear and sequential process of software devlopment
where each phase like Requirements,Planning and Designing, Implementation
Deployment,Testing, and maintance must be completed to the next.once the 
phase is finished going back is very deficult and costly.
